How to Fill Out Reimbursement Form

  1. Download this pdf: "Reimbursement Request Form"
  2. Reimbursements requests must include a completed "Reimbursement Request Form" and a copy of the "Invoice/Receipt" for the purchase to be reimbursed
  3. During a General Meeting, submit "Reimbursement Request Form" and "Invoice/Receipt" to the President
  4. Board will vote on approval of Reimbursement
  5. All disbursements from a club bank account shall be made within 7 days of approval
